# ⛏️ C-Programming with Minecraft

This is an **experiment** to help me get programming into my head by using **Minecraft**. Minecraft is a huge passion of mine, and as a beginner, C can be incredibly abstract. 

> 🧠 **Note:** My ADHD brain isn't always a help there, either.

### The Challenge
When I learn, I want to know exactly what **every single dot and bracket** is responsible for. I often get lost in the **depths of the details**. I love these rabbit holes, but unfortunately, my studies don't give me that kind of time.

### The Strategy
That’s why I’m always looking for the most **creative and interesting** ways to learn:
* **Dopamine Matters:** When things are too boring, my dopamine simply doesn't cooperate. 
* **The Goal:** I’m curious to see where this journey takes me.
